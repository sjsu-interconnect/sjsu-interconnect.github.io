Domain Field Electives

(Catalog 2024 Fall onwards)

Students may pick any courses that are core major requirements in one program. Here are some examples.

  • Students interested in Climate Science may choose ENVS 119 and ENVS 133 to fulfill this requirement. Note that ENVS 119 will also fulfill the area R requirement.
  • Students interested in Economics may choose ECON 1A and ECON 1B.
  • Students interested in Linguistics may choose LING 101 and LING 111.
  • Students interested in Sociology may choose SOCI 1 and SOCI 116.
  • Students interested in Antropology may choose ANTH 13 and ANTH 168.

interconnect lab

  • interconnect lab
  • genya.ishigaki@sjsu.edu

    SJSU interconnect lab, led by Professor Genya Ishigaki, researches next-generation telecommunication networks, with an emphasis on developing combinatorial optimization and machine learning techniques. Our ultimate goal is to propose adaptive and accountable algorithms for autonomous network resource coordination.
