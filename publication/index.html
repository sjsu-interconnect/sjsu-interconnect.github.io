interconnect lab

  • interconnect lab
  • genya.ishigaki@sjsu.edu

    SJSU interconnect lab, led by Genya Ishigaki, researches next-generation telecommunication networks, with an emphasis on developing combinatorial optimization and machine learning techniques. Our ultimate goal is to propose adaptive and accountable algorithms for autonomous network resource coordination.
